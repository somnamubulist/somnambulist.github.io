Hi, I'm Ali Khan, a master's student in Computer Science and machine learning at UC Berkeley. I was born and raised in Pakistan, where I first developed an interest in data science when I worked as a financial analyst. In 2016 I immigrated to the US to pursue a career as a developer in the fintech industry, I am passionate about Member of Black in AI and excited about inclusive technology. My long-term goal is to conduct research and develop technology that can create a positive social impact.
